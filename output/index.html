Interesting Facts

  • Carbon undergoes nuclear fusion reactions in heavy stars to make neon, magnesium and oxygen.

    Sourcehttps://www.chemicool.com/elements/carbon.html

  • Most life prefers 'lighter' carbon isotopes; notably carbon-12 over carbon-13. This fact is especially helpful in identifying fossils.

    Source PBS Eons

  • The carbon atoms in your body were all once part of the carbon dioxide fraction of the atmosphere.

    Sourcehttps://www.chemicool.com/elements/carbon.html
